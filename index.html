about the author
andy d. is a san diego–based multimedia artist, filmmaker, and live-cinema director.
they work in public programming for the san diego public library and the digital gym cinema, focusing on how media and community shape cultural access.
andy is the co-founder of cable two, an experimental public television network, creates art and writing under </body>, and started intertitle.
their practice centers on treating visual art as civic experiences and integrating creativity into everyday life.
