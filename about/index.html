about intertitle
context for the moving image

this page is still under construction.
intertitle is run by an autodidact formalist who lacks brains but makes up for it in persistence. the work centers on form-focused and non-textual poetic criticism in film and theatre, and on building language for live media as a serious art form.
more than anything, this is an attempt to write my way into clearer understanding of what i love.
everything here is public domain and free for anyone to explore.
